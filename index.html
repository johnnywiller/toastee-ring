How the ring is arranged

The goal is that every experienced member holds hands with newer members on both sides — so when someone has a question, they can turn to either neighbour and find guidance.

The algorithm works in three steps:

1. Sort by level (highest first)
Maria lv5 · Janine lv4 · Marie-Pierre lv3 · Nabeelah lv2 · Johnny lv1

2. Split in half
High half → Maria lv5, Janine lv4, Marie-Pierre lv3
Low half  → Nabeelah lv2, Johnny lv1

3. Interleave into the ring
Maria lv5 → Nabeelah lv2 → Janine lv4 → Johnny lv1 → Marie-Pierre lv3 → (back to Maria)

Every high-level member ends up flanked by lower-level ones, and vice versa.
